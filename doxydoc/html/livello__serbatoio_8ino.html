#define VERSION   "v0.12"

Software for water tank level monitoring.

This Software measures and monitors the water level in a tank using an ultrasonic distance sensor. It shows essential information (such as remaining liters and fill percentage) in a 16x2 LCD and warns about low water level using a LED.

Pinout and links:

  • LCD (1602A):
    • Vss : GND
    • Vdd : +5V
    • Vo : OUT Potentiometer (for contrast regulation)
    • RS : DIGITAL 12
    • RW : GND (Write)
    • E : DIGITAL 11
    • DB4, DB5, DB6, DB7
    • 4 , 5 , 6 , 7 (DIGITAL)
